HOW TO COOK PILAU

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 tablespoon of coriander
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of cloves
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Depending on what spice you like more you can add or reduce some from the mix.
  • Now that you have your masala lets get into the recipe.
  • 1/4 kg of boneless cubed lamb This can be replaced with goat or beef
  • 2 large onions finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of Fresh ginger
  • Pilau masala/mix
  • 2 large grated Carrots
  • 1 1/2 cups of clean rice
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 3 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil.

    • INSTRUCTIONS

  • On medium high heat and in 2 tablespoons of oil, saut√© the chopped onions until they soften.
  • Add in the cubed meat, stir the pieces into the onions and cover for about 5 minutes, until it starts to dry up and the meat starts browning.
  • At this point the meat is not cooked through. Its cooking on high heat to get a nice brown that adds to the flavor. The meat will cook completely as the rice boils.
  • Add in the ginger,garlic and 2 tablespoons of the pilau mix. Allow this to cook for about 2 minutes while stirring.
  • Reduce the heat to low and Add in the grated carrots and the clean rice.
  • Stir the rice and carrots until the rice is well coated in the spices.
  • Add in the water and cover tightly with foil or a proper lid. Let this simmer on low heat for about 15 minutes until it starts crackling up to indicate that its dry.
  • Take it off the heat and let it rest for about 5 minutes, fluff up the rice and serve.
